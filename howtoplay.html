How To Play

Here's a short and simple guide on how to play the game.

Once we have all the requirements for this game, we can now proceed to play. Here's how to play

  • The deck will consist of 5 Kings, 5 Queens, 5 Aces, and 2 Jokers.
  • Before the deck is distributed - the table will be given the main card to discard. Sample: King's Table will require players to discard Kings if they have it.
  • Each player will be given 5 cards once the table is decided.
  • A player is eliminated in a Russian Roulette fashion where they will pick a paper / card and it will determine whether they are eliminated or not.
  • One out of 6 cards/papers will say Eliminated.

The goal of the game is to eliminate all other players by bluffing or calling out their lie. As there are a limited number of cards, you can predict your opponent's lie if you have the cards for the type of table.
