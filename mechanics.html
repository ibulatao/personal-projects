The Mechanics

Here are the mechanics of the game

The mechanics of the game is simple. It's a game that uses wits, bluffs and deception. Here are the things needed for us to play the game:

  • 2-4 people
  • Two decks of playing cards or a virtual machine that has the game
  • Stakes that are decided by the players or the standard one in the game

As soon as you have all of these things readily available, you can now get started with setting up the game.
